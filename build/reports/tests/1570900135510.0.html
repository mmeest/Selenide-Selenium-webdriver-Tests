    Unable to log in?

    If you are unable to log in, we invite you to check the following settings and make sure, that

    • right/correct identification method is chosen;
    • correct User ID is entered;
    • “Caps Lock” function is currently turned off;
    • font is not turned on Cyrillic (Russian letters);

    If you use Smart-ID, make sure that you have an active account in Smart-ID app and your mobile phone has Internet available (connection). Make sure that PIN1 is entered in Smart-ID app within 2 minutes of appearance of the control code.

    If you use new type of PIN-calculator, make sure that during authorization you enter APPLI 1 code. If also your spouse or colleagues use PIN-calculator, make sure that you use the right one.

    If you are using ID card, make sure that your computer is prepared for working with ID card (ID card software has been installed and ID card reader is working), and correct PIN is entered.

    Home insurance

    Exclusions

    Exclusions Exceptions
    Damage caused by long-term processes, normal wear and tear, rotting or corrosion.

    We will indemnify you for the loss if one of the abovementioned reasons leads to a fire, explosion or pipe leak, but we do not indemnify the part of the insured object that caused the loss.

    Example

    If a corroded pipe breaks and water damages the interior finish, we will indemnify you for the restoration of the interior finish, but not for the broken pipe.

    Condensation, humidity, long-term variation in temperature, exposure to light, long-term impact of atmospheric conditions or chemicals on the insured object or a part thereof.
    Mould, dry rot, fungal diseases or bacteria on the insured object or a part thereof.  
    Activities of insects, pests, rodents, birds and domestic animals. We will indemnify window glass broken as a result of the activities of birds and animals or a loss caused by fire.
    Defective construction or repair work; calculation, planning or design error; or use of defective or unsuitable building materials.

    We will indemnify you for the loss if one of the abovementioned reasons leads to a sudden and unexpected event, but we do not indemnify the part of the insured object that caused such loss.

    Example

    • If a chimney has not been constructed in accordance with the design requirements and, therefore, leads to a fire, we will indemnify the damage caused to the structure, but we will not replace the chimney.
    • If the wall tiles in your bathroom are fixed with an insufficient amount of tile glue and fall off because of this, thus damaging the sink, we will indemnify the damage caused to the sink, but not the wall tiles.
    • If, due to defective construction, water or humidity get in between the thermal insulation and plaster, no indemnity is paid as this is not a sudden event.
    • If the insulation material of the structure has been poorly installed and this has caused thermal bridges and condensation or mould damage to the interior finish of the structure, we will not replace the insulation material as the damage was not caused by a sudden or unexpected event. No indemnity is paid for damage to the interior finish since condensation and mould are not covered by the insurance.
    Subsidence, elevation, movement, expansion, wear of materials and vibration of the structure, its foundations, walls or other structural elements. We will indemnify you for a loss caused by:
    • movement of the surface or vibration caused by construction work performed by third parties whose liability is proven
    • rise of surface water or a flood.
    Water entering the structure through a broken construction. We presume that the construction element is broken when the external barriers of the structure that are designed to prevent water from entering the structure fail to meet their purpose. We will indemnify you for loss caused by:
    • water entering the structure due to poor construction if such a loss is caused by a sudden and unexpected external event and the damage has been identified
    • first leakage of water through roof or wall structures that damage the interior finish or home contents.
    Loss of home contents. We will indemnify the loss of home contents due to theft or robbery. Theft is deprivation of home contents as a result of a break-in to the place of insurance, or outside thereof. Robbery means deprivation of home contents accompanied by the use of violence that represents a threat to a person’s life and health or the threat to use violence.
    Failure of or damage to home appliances or devices (e.g. boiler or central heating boiler) that has not been caused by external events.
    The use of the insured object in a manner not complying with the purpose or function established by the manufacturer. This includes testing or intentional overloading of or performing experiments on machines and equipment.  
    Nuclear weapon, nuclear fuel, nuclear energy or radioactive substance or any other source of radiation or the explosion of a nuclear or radioactive substance.  

    The following are not subject to indemnification:

    • Loss and damage which occurred before the Insurance contract was entered into or the occurrence of which was obvious when the Insurance contract was entered into.
    • Expenses incurred in the maintenance, repair and elimination of minor external defects, such as stains on carpets and sofa covers; scratches on painted, enamelled and polished surfaces; cracks in walls and changes in colour or odours.
    • Damage and costs incurring during scheduled work such as changing of radiators, piping or windows.
    • Loss of income, financial liabilities, moral damage or other costs not agreed upon.

    • Your home property (e.g. furniture, appliances, bicycle) is secured both at home and across Estonia.

    Why insure your home with Swedbank?

    • Convenience – Your monthly payments will be deducted from your current account and your contract will be extended each year automatically.
    • Broad-ranging cover – All-risk insurance covers just about any event that could cause damage to your home (fires, storms, floods, burglaries, vandalism etc.).
    • Protection for your property – The insurance covers not only contents inside your home, but also garden furniture, lawnmowers and anything you have in storage.
    • Liability insurance – Any harm caused to third parties is also covered, such as water damage to a downstairs apartment during renovations, or broken panes in a greenhouse caused by a stray ball.

    To enter into an insurance agreement, send us your details and we will get back to you within a week.

    What home insurance covers?

    • Home insurance provides cover for the building, home property and cases where you have caused damages to other people (e.g. your neighbours).
    • In the case of an insured event involving your home, you will be reimbursed for costs related to restoring your home.
    • In the case of an insured event involving home property, you will be reimbursed for the repair costs or the costs of repurchasing.
    • If you or one of your family members has cause damages to a third party (e.g. your neighbour), the resulting property damage or personal injury will be compensated.
    • List of the events when our insurance doesn’t cover

    For example, Swedbank home insurance will cover damages in case of following:

    • Fire. Home with fire damage will be restored
    • Hail and wind. Damage will be covered regardless of storm strenght
    • Thunder and power surges. We cover damages caused by thunder or power surges.
    • Plumbing and flood.
    • Theft and robbery. In the case of the theft or robbery of or damage to home contents, we will indemnify the costs of repairs or reacquisition of a similar item. Restrictions are set out in the conditions.

    Insurable value

    Building’s insurable value is the cost of restoring the building to previous state - restoration value. Insurable value of home contents means the acquisition cost of items included in home contents when brand new. The insurable value of certain items is the residual or market value of the item prior to the insured event. Restrictions are set out in the conditions.

    Liability insurance

    Liability insurance has you covered if you or someone in your family causes harm or damage to a third party. This cover is also valid outside of your home anywhere in Estonia.

    Buildings

    By insuring your home, all of the structures that form part of it are covered, as are the essential parts of the building (including built-in lighting and sanitary systems), its technical systems (including heating pumps and communications lines), its interior finish (including kitchen furniture, but excluding kitchen appliances) and built-in furniture. The interior finish of a terraced house and the essential parts of the building located within the terraced house are insured up to the interior finish of the neighbouring house.

    Insurance of a building or part of a building also covers facilities. Facilities are separate structures that are not buildings. They must be located on the same plot of land as the building or part of a building and permanently attached to the ground, such as fences, outdoor lighting fixtures, flagpoles, wells, kennels, pools, playgrounds (incl. playhouses), shelters and greenhouses of up to 20 m².

    By insuring your apartment, the living space is covered, as are the essential parts of the apartment (including built-in lighting and sanitary systems), its technical systems, its interior finish (including kitchen furniture, but excluding kitchen appliances) and built-in furniture and any storage room that belongs to the apartment but is located outside of the apartment.

    Home contents

    By insuring your home contents, all of the property belonging to or at the disposal of the owner of the home or apartment or the individual(s) living there on a permanent basis is covered, both within and beyond the confines of the insured location.

    When taking out home contents insurance you get to choose the extent of the cover. And we don’t ask you for a detailed list of the items you want to insure.

    Insurance on home proposes a wide range of insurance cover and thus you may be certain that your home contents are covered against almost any unexpected and fortuitous accidents. There are only some exclusions which are not eligible for cover.

    Up-to-date renovation

    After the insured event we cover the renovation costs of your flat. We guarantee the use of up-to-date building- and furnishing materials in the process of renovation. In the event of the loss of home in fire or for some other reason, we shall finance the construction of a new home.

    The insurance of the kitchen furniture together with the building

    Modern kitchen furniture and other built-in furniture cost a lot. By insuring the above mentioned furniture together with your flat or house you will notably reduce the insurance premium on your home contents – you will have a more favourable insurance premium.

    The rent compensation for a temporary dwelling

    After a insured event you may need to temporarily live away from your home – until your home is restored. We shall cover the rent for the temporary dwelling to ease your nervousness and expenses related to the renovation of the flat. For the Insurance on home clients this additional cover is free of charge.

    Loss of rental income

    If you have given an insured building or apartment on lease and the lease is terminated due to an insured event, we will indemnify you for the loss of rental income until the building or apartment is reconstructed, but for not longer than 12 months.

    The sum insured after the loss is not reduced

    We shall guarantee that the agreed on sum insured shall continue to be effective on your house after the loss as well.

    You do not have to pay any additional premium to reinstate the original sum insured.

    Restoration of a building without considering the wear and tear

    Damages caused to a structure are indemnified on the basis of the reinstatement value of the property. We do not consider the wear and tear of a building when it is restored.

    Facilities

    Insurance of a building or part of a building also covers facilities. Facilities are separate structures that are not buildings. They must be located on the same plot of land as the building or part of a building and permanently attached to the ground, such as fences, outdoor lighting fixtures, flagpoles, wells, kennels, pools, playgrounds (incl. playhouses), shelters and greenhouses of up to 20 m².

    A home security system is not required

    We do not require you to have a home security system in order to enter into an insurance agreement. Nor does this affect the size of your insurance payments or the amount for which you are compensated in the event of a claim.

    Loss assessment

    The instruction for the steps to be taken, which you receive from the average adjuster of Swedbank, depends on the case. In order to assess the amount of loss caused to your home the average adjuster will make an appointment to look over your home. During the visit the average adjuster will ask you to fill a claim for damages form. We may ask for additional documents necessary for making a decision about the indemnity, e.g. explanatory statement, a copy of the notification submitted to the police, receipts in proof of purchasing items etc.

    Preparation of renovation

    After receiving the notification of loss and claim, and looking over the home, Swedbank will process the decision of indemnity and dispatch a renovation company an approval for work. Thereafter the renovation company will agree with you about the time of work.

    Payment of excess

    The excess for each insured event is 190 euros. In the event of monetary compensation, this will be deducted from the total amount of the indemnity. If the compensation is paid directly to the repair company, you will receive a separate invoice, which must be paid as agreed before or after the repairs.

    Should the keys be stolen and locks broken we will replace your door-locks

    In case your keys are stolen or the locks of your doors are broken, it is necessary to replace the locks in order to avoid further damage. The costs involved are covered by us.

    The rental costs of a temporary dwelling

    During the period of renovation it usually is impossible to live at home. We cover the rental costs of the temporary dwelling but for no more than 12 months. Temporary dwelling place may be a hotel, leased flat or -house.

    The costs of after damage demolition and debris/rubbish transportation

    The costs of demolition and debris/rubbish transportation, as the result of water- or fire caused damage, are covered.

    Notify of damage

    Should an accident occur at home, notify about it immediately:
    • to the police, in case your home and home contents have been broken or a theft has occurred. In the event your home is under the protection of a security company who has received no call in the time the damage was caused, notify them, too;
    • to the rescue board, in case there is a fire or explosion;
    • to the rescue board or house administrator, in case you are unable to close the taps of the broken circulation system.

    Thereafter immediately inform our Claims Handling department of the event and send us all the information concerning the loss event.

    The representative of Swedbank receives the notification of claim and advises how to act on the spot of action and how the claim will be adjusted.
